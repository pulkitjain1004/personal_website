About Me

To tell it's own story

"The idea is to go from numbers to information to understanding", Hans Rosling. And I am passionate about designing data-driven stories. Uncovering and telling visual stories facilitated by access to large datasets and data analysis tools. Trends, comparisons, relationships and sometimes surprising or counter intutive data, everything is present there, waiting to be found!

I am a great believer in growth, bold thinking, and community service. Today’s technology landscape provides advanced options that can improve business performance in leaps and bounds. I am currently pursuing master’s in Industrial Engineering at Texas A&M University. In the next few years I will like to comprehend the vital commercial details of the Business. And my master's will help me leverage the right technologies to improve the business productivity.

Currently, I am looking for full-time opportunities in areas of Business Analytics, Data Science, Project Management and Consulting. My work history includes multi-dimensional job roles, including machine learning, data mining, project management, supply-chain management and non-profits. My present work focuses on public health research through data analytics.
